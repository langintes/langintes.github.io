Animals in Icelandic songs

Many traditional Icelandic songs feature local animals, hinting at their relationship with humans and the mythology around them.

This page covers a few that have left a mark on Icelandic culture.

Raven Fox Horse

The fox in Icelandic folksongs

The arctic fox was the only mammal living in Iceland when human settlers arrived in the ninth century.

He mainly eats birds and insects but has been known to kill lambs also.

A song about a fox

Siggi var úti

In the song, a young boy named Siggi is out in the fields looking after some sheep.

Siggi is frigthened when a scary fox comes along, leading him to run for it, leaving the sheep he was supposed to look out for.
